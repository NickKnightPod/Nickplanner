Map out the plan

One section per line: Section name | hours (e.g. Draft workflow doc | 3). Hours default to 2 if you leave them off. Each section becomes a to-do — the planner spreads their deadlines evenly between now and the Rock's due date, then slots them into your admin days for you automatically.
